About Dingyunn

Dingyunn: Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where your goal is simple: stay alive as long as possible against endless waves of monsters. As you progress, the challenges become more intense, testing your skills and strategic thinking.

Set in a mysterious world overrun by creatures of the night, you'll take on the role of a brave survivor equipped with unique abilities. Collect power-ups, unlock new weapons, and upgrade your character to face increasingly difficult hordes of enemies.

How to Play

  • Movement: Use WASD keys or arrow keys to move your character around the map.
  • Automatic Attack: Your character will automatically attack nearby enemies.
  • Level Up: Collect experience gems dropped by defeated monsters to level up.
  • Choose Upgrades: Select power-ups and weapon upgrades when leveling up.
  • Survive: Avoid getting overwhelmed by monsters and survive as long as possible.

The longer you survive, the more challenging the game becomes. Monsters will spawn more frequently and with increased health and damage. Strategic movement and smart upgrade choices are key to surviving the later stages.

Tips and Tricks

  1. Prioritize movement speed upgrades early to help you avoid enemy attacks.
  2. Don't stay in one place for too long - keep moving to avoid being surrounded.
  3. Focus on upgrading a few weapons to maximum level rather than spreading upgrades across many weapons.
  4. Area-of-effect weapons are particularly effective when enemies start to swarm.
  5. Health regeneration upgrades become increasingly valuable in the later stages.
